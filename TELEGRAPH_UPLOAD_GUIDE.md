# How to Upload Image to Telegraph

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Option 1: Direct Upload (Easiest)

1. **Go to https://telegra.ph**
2. **Click anywhere in the editor** (the white text area)
3. **Press Enter** to create a new line
4. **Click the image icon** (usually appears in the toolbar, or try Ctrl+V / Cmd+V to paste)
5. **OR drag and drop** your image file directly onto the page
6. **OR click "Upload"** if you see an upload button

### Option 2: Using the Upload Button

1. Look for an **upload button** or **image icon** in the interface
2. Click it
3. Select your file: `WhatsApp Image 2025-08-16 at 00.09.18.jpeg`
4. Wait for upload to complete

### Option 3: Paste Image

1. Open your image file on your computer
2. Copy it (Cmd+C on Mac, Ctrl+C on Windows)
3. Go to telegraph
4. Click in the editor
5. Paste (Cmd+V on Mac, Ctrl+V on Windows)

## After Uploading:

1. The image will appear in the editor
2. **Right-click on the uploaded image**
3. Select **"Copy image address"** or **"Copy image URL"**
4. You'll get a URL like: `https://telegra.ph/file/abc123def456.jpg`
5. **This is the URL you need for Netlify's PIC variable!**

## Important Notes:

- You don't need to publish the article - just upload the image
- The image URL will work even if you don't publish
- Make sure to copy the **image URL**, not the article URL
